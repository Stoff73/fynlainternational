## Step 1: Add Your Data

**Headline**: Tell Fynla What You've Got (And What You Owe)

**Body**: Start by adding your key financial data. You don't need everything on day one — start with the basics and build up over time.

**What to add**:
- Pensions — workplace, personal, SIPP, state pension forecast
- Property — home value, mortgage balance
- Savings & investments — ISAs, savings accounts, investment accounts
- Insurance — life, income protection, critical illness
- Debts — mortgage, loans, credit cards

**Time**: 15-20 minutes for the initial setup. 5 minutes to add each additional item later.

**Reassurance**: You enter data manually — no bank logins or API connections needed. You stay in full control.

---

## Step 2: See Your Picture

**Headline**: Your Complete Financial Dashboard

**Body**: Once your data is in, Fynla shows you everything:

- **Net worth** — what you own minus what you owe, tracked over time
- **Retirement projection** — when you could afford to stop working
- **Protection gaps** — whether your family is adequately covered
- **IHT exposure** — what HMRC would take from your estate (Premium)
- **Pension overview** — all your pots in one place with growth projections

**Callout**: This is the "aha moment" — the first time most people see their entire financial life on one screen.

## Step 3: Model Your Future

**Headline**: Pull the Levers. See What Changes.

**Body**: The real power of Fynla is scenario modelling. Ask "what if" and get instant answers:

- What if I increase pension contributions by 2%?
- What if I retire at 58 instead of 62?
- What if I overpay my mortgage by £200/month?
- What if I gift £50,000 to my children now?
- What if markets crash by 30% in year one of retirement?

Every change updates your dashboard in real time. No waiting for an adviser. No spreadsheet gymnastics. Just answers.
