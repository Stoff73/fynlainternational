What do I need to start my journey?

To get the most from Fynla, have these details ready. You don't need everything at once — you can always come back and add more later.

About You

Basic personal details

  • Date of birth
  • Employment status
  • Marital status
  • Number of dependants

Income & Tax

Your earnings and tax details

  • Annual salary or income
  • Tax code (from payslip)
  • Student loan plan type
  • Any additional income

Monthly Spending

A rough idea of your outgoings

  • Rent or mortgage payment
  • Bills and utilities
  • Food and essentials
  • Subscriptions

Savings & Assets

What you have right now

  • Savings account balances
  • ISA balances
  • Workplace pension value
  • Any other savings

Debts & Liabilities

What you currently owe

  • Student loan balance
  • Credit card balances
  • Personal loans
  • Overdraft amounts

Your Goals

What you want to achieve

  • Emergency fund target
  • Savings goals
  • Short-term priorities
  • What matters most to you
Start your journey
