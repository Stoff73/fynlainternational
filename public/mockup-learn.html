What is an ISA?

An Individual Savings Account (ISA) is a tax-efficient savings or investment wrapper available to UK residents. You can save or invest up to £20,000 per tax year across different ISA types without paying income tax or capital gains tax on the returns.

There are four main types: Cash ISA, Stocks and Shares ISA, Lifetime ISA, and Innovative Finance ISA. Each has its own rules, but they all share the same annual allowance.
